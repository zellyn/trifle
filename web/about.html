About This Project

What is Trifling?

Trifling is where you write Python code and watch it run—right in your browser! No downloads, no setup, no complicated installations. Just open it and start coding.

Your Code, Your Computer

Everything you create stays on your computer. Your Trifles (that's what we call your projects!) are saved in your browser, so they work even when you're offline. They're private and they're yours.

What Can You Do?

  • Write games, calculators, and fun programs
  • Code anywhere—even on a plane with no WiFi
  • Make mistakes safely and learn by doing
